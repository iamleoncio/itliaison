Closing Tickets

Easy Way to Close Tickets

USING GROUP UPDATES

Filter Status and Location

1st Step

Ifilter po muna ang mga tickets na for assignment at location.depende kung anong area ang hawak nyo. then click EDIT

CLICK SAVE

2nd Step

Click save para maassigned sayo ang ticket then click NEXT gawin mo ito sa lahat ng ticket na nafilter mo

Group Updates

3rd Step

after mo magrab lahat ng ticket. filter mo lahat ng nagrab mo , checkk all tickets then click GROUP UPDATES

Change Status to In Progress

4th Step

Yan ang lalabas after maclick ang GROUP UPDATES then change status to IN PROGRESS then click SAVE

Change Status to filter

5th Steps

After mo masave ay palitan mo yung filter mo na In Progress sa status kasi naging in progress na yung mga tickets

Resolving

6th Step

After mafilter lahat ng naka in Progress na sayu ay click GROUP UPDATES

Change Status to RESOLVED then Add Resolution

7th Step

After maclick ang GROOUP UPDATES change status to RESOLVED add RESOLUTUON then click SAVE after masave ay may nakikita ka pa rin . just reload kasi mabagal maagload ang kaseya.
